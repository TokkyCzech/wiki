# Beta Testing

## Create your items
Normal item :
`$item [VNUM] [quantity] [rarity] [upgrade]`

**Examples:**

Item creation: `$item [VNUM] [quantity]`

Equipment creation: `$item [VNUM] [quantity] [rarity] [upgrade]`

SP creation: `$item [VNUM] [upgrade]`

Or alternatively:
`$sp [vnum] [upgrade]`
